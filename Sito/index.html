Who we are?

Adidas is a German multinational corporation based in Herzogenaurach.

Adidas produces footwear, clothing and other sporting goods, for professional, amateur or leisure activities. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe and the second largest in the world. The company's products are all identified by the characteristic three parallel and obliquely arranged stripes, which may recall a shape of the ancient Greek letter sampi. This identification also appears in the company's official logo.
